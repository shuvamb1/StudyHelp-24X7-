By organizing materials semester-wise and crowdsourcing contributions from the student community, we ensure that every student has the tools they need to prepare effectively and excel in their coursework. Community contribution is at the heart of our platform.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about StudyHelp 24x7

Are the study materials verified?
While we rely on community contributions, our moderators try to review the notes for general relevance and accuracy before publishing. However, we always recommend cross-checking with official college syllabus and professors.
How can I contribute my own notes?
You can easily upload your notes by navigating to the "Contribute" page. Make sure your files are in PDF, DOCX, or PPTX format. Once uploaded, they will be sent for review.
Is the platform officially affiliated with St. Xavier's College?
No, StudyHelp 24x7 is an independent student-led initiative created by and for the students of St. Xavier's College.
Is it free to use?
Yes, completely! StudyHelp 24x7 is and always will be free for all students of St. Xavier's College. Our goal is to democratize academic resources for everyone.
