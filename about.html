About The Project

This website is a project made by Shuvam Bhattacharya, Student of computer science of St Xavier's College for the benefits of all the students of St Xavier's College.

Our Mission

To democratize access to high-quality academic resources, fostering a collaborative learning environment where every student has the tools they need to succeed academically.

Our Vision

To become the ultimate, most trusted academic companion for every student crossing the threshold of St. Xavier's College, ensuring no one ever struggles alone with their coursework.

StudyHelp 24x7

Empowering students of St. Xavier's College, Kolkata with a 24x7 accessible, premium academic resource hub.
