About The Project

This website is a project made by Shuvam Bhattacharya, Student of computer science of St Xavier's College for the benefits of all the students of St Xavier's College. What started as a simple idea to share notes among classmates quickly evolved into a comprehensive academic resource hub serving hundreds of students across multiple departments.

StudyHelp 24x7 was born out of a genuine need — students often struggled to find reliable study materials, especially during exam season. By creating a centralized platform, we aim to eliminate the stress of searching for resources and let students focus on what truly matters: learning.

Our Mission

To democratize access to high-quality academic resources, fostering a collaborative learning environment where every student has the tools they need to succeed academically. We believe that financial constraints or lack of connections should never be a barrier to education.

We are committed to maintaining a platform that is free, accessible, and constantly improving through community contributions. Every note uploaded, every question paper shared, and every resource contributed helps build a stronger academic ecosystem for current and future students.

Our Vision

To become the ultimate, most trusted academic companion for every student crossing the threshold of St. Xavier's College, ensuring no one ever struggles alone with their coursework. We envision a campus where knowledge flows freely and students uplift each other.

Looking ahead, we plan to expand our offerings with AI-powered study tools, interactive mock tests, and personalized learning paths — all designed to make studying smarter, more efficient, and more collaborative than ever before.
